Welcome to Trippy user guide

Loging in

Step 1: Click on the Log In button in the top rigth corner.

Step 2: Insert your login credentials (username or email and password) into the left form.

Step 3: Press Log In button and you will be redirected to the main page.

Login form




Registrating a new account

Step 1: Click on the Log In button in the top rigth corner.

Step 2: Insert your credentials (username, email and password) into the right form.

Step 3: Retype your password.

Step 4: Press Create button.

Register form




Forgetting your account's password

Step 1: Click on the Log In button in the top rigth corner.

Step 2: Click on "Forgot pasword?" link below Log In button on the left form.

Forgot password link

Step 3: You will be redirected to a new page where you insert your account's email address.

Step 4: Press Send button.

Sending information to email

Step 5: You will recieve an email with the link to change your old password.




Changing your account's old password

Step 1: Log In to your account

Step 2: Click on your username in the navigation bar at the top of the page.

Username in the navigation bar

Step 3: You will be redirected to your profile page.

Step 4: Insert your old and your new password.

Step 5: Retype your new password.

Step 6: Press Change button.

Profile page form




How to use Trippy trip planner

Step 1: Log In to your account

Step 2: You will be redirected to the home page.

Step 3: Type in your starting and ending destination and press Plan your trip button.

Starting and ending destination

Step 4: On the next page chose your stops on the right side of the screen (for multiple stops hold down "control").

Selecting stops

Step 5: Press Show button.

Step 6: Your route will be generated on the map and route information on the right of the page.
